## Method 1: Track ID Card Status via SMS

The SMS method is the easiest and most widely used way to check NADRA ID card status in Pakistan.

**Steps:**

1. Open the SMS app on your mobile phone.
2. Type your **13-digit CNIC number** (without dashes).
3. Send the SMS to **8300**.

You will receive a reply from NADRA within a few seconds indicating the current status of your ID card application.

**Note:** This service is available on all networks — Jazz, Telenor, Ufone, and Zong. Standard SMS charges may apply.

---

## Method 2: Track via NADRA Official Website

NADRA provides an online tracking portal where you can check the status of your Smart National Identity Card.

**Steps:**

1. Visit the official NADRA website: [https://www.nadra.gov.pk](https://www.nadra.gov.pk)
2. Navigate to the **"Track Your Application"** or **"SNIC Status"** section.
3. Enter your **13-digit CNIC number**.
4. Complete the CAPTCHA verification.
5. Click **Submit** to view your current application status.

The portal displays statuses such as "Under Process," "Printed," "Dispatched," or "Ready for Collection."

---

## Method 3: NADRA e-Services Portal

NADRA's dedicated e-services portal offers a more detailed tracking experience along with additional online services.

**Steps:**

1. Go to [https://id.nadra.gov.pk](https://id.nadra.gov.pk)
2. Register or log in to your account.
3. Under the **My Applications** section, select your application.
4. View the real-time status and processing stage of your ID card.

This portal is especially useful if you applied online and want to manage multiple applications or track delivery.

---

## Method 4: NADRA Helpline (Call Center)

You can speak directly to a NADRA representative to inquire about your ID card status.

**Helpline Number:** **1800** (Toll-Free)

**Steps:**

1. Dial **1800** from any mobile or landline.
2. Select your preferred language (Urdu or English).
3. Choose the option for ID card status inquiry.
4. Provide your **CNIC number** when prompted.
5. The representative will inform you about your application's current stage.

The helpline operates during business hours, typically Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 5 PM PKT.

---

## Method 5: Visit Your Nearest NADRA Registration Centre (NRC)

If digital methods are not accessible or you need further clarification, you can visit any NADRA Registration Centre (NRC) in person.

**Steps:**

1. Locate the nearest NRC using the NADRA website or Google Maps.
2. Bring your **original token/receipt** issued at the time of application.
3. Approach the inquiry counter and provide your receipt or CNIC number.
4. A NADRA officer will check your application status on the system.

This method is particularly helpful when there is a data mismatch or when your application has been on hold for verification.

---

## Method 6: NADRA Mobile App

NADRA has introduced a mobile application for citizens to manage their identity-related services on the go.

**Steps:**

1. Download the **NADRA e-Sahulat** or **NADRA ID** app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
2. Log in or register using your CNIC.
3. Navigate to the application tracking section.
4. Enter your details to view your ID card status instantly.

The app also lets you apply for renewal, update personal information, and schedule appointments at NRCs.

---

## Method 7: Pakistan Post Office Tracking (For Home Delivery)

If you opted for **home delivery** when applying for your SNIC, your card is dispatched through Pakistan Post. You can track the delivery separately.

**Steps:**

1. Note the **tracking number** provided by NADRA at the time of dispatch.
2. Visit [https://www.pakpost.gov.pk](https://www.pakpost.gov.pk) or contact your local post office.
3. Enter the tracking number to check the delivery status.

Home delivery is available for Ordinary and Urgent card categories and is a convenient option for those unable to visit NRCs.

## Understanding NADRA ID Card Application Statuses

When you check your ID card status, you may encounter the following stages:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Under Process** | Application received and being reviewed |
| **Data Verification** | NADRA is verifying your provided information |
| **Printed** | Your ID card has been printed successfully |
| **Dispatched** | Card has been sent to your NRC or postal address |
| **Ready for Collection** | Card is available at your designated NRC |
| **Delivered** | Card has been delivered to your home address |
| **On Hold** | Application has an issue requiring attention |

## Tips to Speed Up Your Application

- Apply for **Executive** processing if you need the card urgently (processing in 2–3 working days).
- Ensure all **biometric data** (fingerprints and photos) are correctly captured.
- Double-check your **address and personal details** before submitting.
- Keep your **application receipt/token number** safe for reference.
- Avoid multiple applications, as duplicate submissions can cause delays.

---

## Common Issues and How to Resolve Them

**Card status stuck on "Under Process" for weeks:**
Visit your nearest NRC with your token. There may be a data discrepancy or a pending verification step that requires your physical presence.

**SMS to 8300 returns no result:**
Ensure you are sending the correct 13-digit CNIC without spaces or dashes. If the problem persists, try the NADRA website or helpline.

**Application marked "On Hold":**
This usually indicates a data mismatch or that additional documents are required. Visit your NRC immediately with all original supporting documents.

**Card dispatched but not received:**
Contact Pakistan Post using your tracking number. If the card has been returned to the NRC, you can collect it in person.
